## Objection Handlers

### "AI will replace us anyway"
**"YJackGameStudio proves the opposite: AI **assists**, humans **direct**. You're not competing with AI; you're wielding it like a team of junior devs who need supervision. Your creative vision, their execution speed."**

### "I don't trust AI-generated code"
**"Neither do we. That's why YJackGameStudio requires unit tests for logic stories, integration tests for multi-system work, and evidence packets for all QA. Trust is earned through validation, not blind faith."**

### "This sounds complicated"
**"Day 1: `/start` guides you through onboarding. Day 2: `/brainstorm` helps you shape a concept. Day 3: `/design-system` writes your first GDD. By week 2, you're implementing stories with `/dev-story`. The ramp is gradual, and you're never alone."**

### "What about IP and licensing?"
**"YJackGameStudio itself is MIT-licensed (permissive, open-source). The AI tools you use (Claude Code, Copilot, etc.) each have their own IP terms — check with your tool vendor. We don't change those terms; we just orchestrate the tools."**

### "I'm not a Unity dev"
**"Perfect! We support Godot and Unreal equally. Unity + YJackCore is one **optional** path. Pick your engine, run `/setup-engine`, and you're set."**
